Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Are the calculators on CalcTools Pro free to use?

Yes, all calculators available on CalcTools Pro are completely free to use. We aim to provide accessible and user-friendly tools for everyone.

How accurate are the calculations?

Our calculators are designed to provide accurate results based on standard formulas and the data you input. However, they are intended for informational purposes only and should not replace professional advice. For critical calculations, always verify with a qualified professional or refer to our Disclaimer.

Do I need to register or create an account to use the calculators?

No, you do not need to register or create an account to use any of the calculators on CalcTools Pro. All tools are accessible directly.

Is my data safe when I use CalcTools Pro?

We take your privacy seriously. Most of our calculators perform calculations directly in your browser and do not store your input data on our servers. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for detailed information on data handling.

Can I use CalcTools Pro on my mobile device?

Yes, CalcTools Pro is designed to be responsive and should work well on most modern mobile devices and tablets, as well as desktop computers.

What types of calculators do you offer?

We offer a variety of calculators for everyday needs, including an Age Calculator, BMI Calculator, Percentage Calculator, EMI Calculator, and Discount Calculator. We are always looking to add more useful tools.

How can I suggest a new calculator or report an issue?

We welcome your feedback! If you have a suggestion for a new calculator or if you've found an issue with an existing one, please contact us through our contact page. We appreciate your help in improving our service.

Do you provide customer support?

While we don't offer live customer support, you can reach out to us via our Contact Us page with any questions or concerns not covered in these FAQs, and we'll do our best to assist you.
